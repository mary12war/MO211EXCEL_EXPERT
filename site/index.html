How to Use This Site

Follow the same three-step cycle on every page so the material feels consistent whether you are learning alone or teaching a class.

1. Preview the objective

Start with the hook, misconception, and mastery steps to understand what the exam task is really asking you to do.

2. Practice in Excel

Open the linked workbook or CSV, reproduce the worked example, and narrate each click aloud to build exam muscle memory.

3. Self-check and repeat

Use the quiz and follow-up prompt to verify understanding, then mark the page complete and move to the next domain.
